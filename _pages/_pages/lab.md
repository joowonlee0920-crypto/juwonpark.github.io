# PARK JUWON Research Laboratory

## Mission

I explore AI-driven environmental technologies to make scientific analysis more accessible and contribute to environmental sustainability.

## Research Philosophy

Research is never finished. It evolves through questions, failures, experiments, and reflection.

## Research Areas

- Artificial Intelligence
- Environmental Monitoring
- Microplastics
- Spectroscopy
- Water Security
- Resource Recovery

## Open Questions

- Can smartphones become scientific instruments?
- Can visible-light spectroscopy classify microplastics?
- Can AI predict water pollution before contamination occurs?
- How can scientific technology become accessible to everyone?

## Failed Experiments

I document failures because they reveal how research actually grows.

## Future Vision

My long-term goal is to develop accessible scientific technologies that help solve environmental problems.
